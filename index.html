About This Page:

Improbale Beef, Inc. has partnered with a molecular biology lab in attempts of finding a bacterial species capable of producing proteins that taste like beef! The study is particularly interested in bacterial species found in our bellybuttons as a potential candidate! This website aggragates demographic information of participants of the study, as well as quantatative information for the bacterial species found. The information is displayed in a horizontal bar graph to help visualize the total distribution, whereas a bubble chart is used to alternatively show distribution and spread of bacterial species found. Each participant is assigned an ID, which they can input below to display their information.
